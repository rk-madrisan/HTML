What We Offer?

MEASI Institute of Information Technology has set out to blur the distinctive line that exists between academics and the industry with revolutionary teaching methods. MIIT makes its students ready for the real world. MIIT-ians are trained to develop their problem-solving skills, communication and other the essential tools.

Participatory Learning & Practical Application

Process that involves activities and projects, learning to understand and solve problems at the individual level

Research & Intelligence

Data analysis & interpretation enabling critical thinking and problem solving capabilities.

Industry Connect & Collaboration

Insight on the functioning of the industry, and enables them to develop conceptual and practical knowledge.

Mentorship & Guidance

Experienced and steadying mentor to rapidly transition from academia to a challenging but exciting world outside.

Expertise & Powerskills

Possess quintessential expertise, viz. leadership, effective communication, critical thinking, creativity and collaborative teamwork.

Faculty

MIIT has a student: faculty ratio of 20:1, the best among the colleges offering MCA in the state. Faculty members in MIIT possess the requisite qualification and have a rich domain experience in the field of Information Technology. Apart from teaching, they engage as resource persons in workshops, seminars, conferences, faculty development and management development programmes.

Senior faculty members additionally render their services as consultants to corporates, who seek their help in analysing and addressing the needs and challenges faced by the industry.

Read More >>

Placements

The Placement Cell facilitates students to explore career options and guides them in identifying potential employers that match their interests, strengths and skill set.

Case studies, real-life projects, group discussions, interactions with industry experts and alumni, all form part of this programme and go a long way in preparing the students for placements.

Continuous interaction with prospective employers is maintained. Placement activities are augmented by regular industry-institution-interface.

Read More

Research & Consultancy

Faculty members at MIIT generate knowledge through research that benefits organizations and society. The knowledge generated has appeared in leading academic journals with high citation index and impact factor. Research carried out by MIIT is used to augment new FDP, MDP and executive education programmes.

MIIT also offers consultancy services to the industry. The research and consultancy activities in MIIT has been gaining momentum in the recent past and has been well received and supported by the industry.

Blended Learning
